Ranno.ai Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 31, 2025

At Ranno.ai, your privacy is paramount. We are committed to creating a running route planner that is not only smart and safe but also respects and protects your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data when you use Ranno.ai.

1. Information We Collect

  • User Input (Natural Language Queries): Processed locally on your device via Gemini Nano; not stored or sent to Ranno.ai servers in a personally identifiable way.
  • Location Data (Approximate/Contextual): Used transiently for route generation; we do not track GPS or store exact coordinates.
  • Non-Personalized Usage Data: Aggregated, anonymized metrics help improve the experience without identifying individuals.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Generate personalized running routes tailored to your requests.
  • Improve Ranno.ai through aggregated analytics.
  • Integrate safety and amenity data to deliver safer, more convenient routes.

3. How We Share Your Information

  • We do not sell or rent personal data.
  • Route requests are sent to Google Maps APIs (subject to Google’s privacy policy).
  • We use public crime and amenity data APIs without sharing personal user information.
  • Information may be shared only when legally required or to protect rights and safety.

4. On-Device AI & Your Privacy

Gemini Nano runs locally via Chrome’s Prompt API, keeping your requests on your device and reducing data transfer.

5. Data Security

We apply reasonable safeguards but cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Choices & Rights

  • Control what you input into the app.
  • Stop using Ranno.ai at any time.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically; revisions will appear here with an updated effective date.
